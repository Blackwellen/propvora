# Propvora

Propvora is a UK property operations, compliance and profit-control platform,
operated by Blackwellen Ltd (registered in England and Wales, No. 16482166),
trading as Propvora. ICO registration ZC160806.
Canonical: https://propvora.com

## Status
Propvora is RELEASING now at V1–V1.5 — the property-operations + compliance core
is live and production-ready. The broader V2 "property operating system"
(marketplace, bookings, customer & supplier workspaces, automations, affiliate
payouts) is rolling out progressively behind feature flags; the functions are
listed below so you know what's coming.

## Target users
UK landlords, self-managing portfolio operators, and letting/managing agencies.

## Property types supported
Single lets, portfolios, HMOs, and managed/agency portfolios.

===================================================================
## AVAILABLE NOW — V1 to V1.5 (releasing)
===================================================================
- Portfolio: properties, units, tenancies, documents
- Compliance: EPC, Gas Safety (CP12), EICR, certificates, inspections, expiry
  reminders and a compliance dashboard — Propvora's core differentiator
- Work & maintenance: tasks, jobs, suppliers, planned maintenance (PPM),
  tenant-reported issues
- Money: income, expenses, invoices, deposits, rent recording, profit & cashflow
  (rent and supplier invoices are recorded FCA-safely — Propvora does not collect
  or hold this money; the landlord/agent is paid directly)
- Contacts: tenants, landlords, suppliers, agents, accountants
- Portals: scoped tenant / landlord / supplier portals (invited access only)
- AI Copilot: workspace-scoped assistance and drafting, with rate/usage caps
- Internationalisation: workspace locale, currency and legal-context settings
  (default UK / GBP / England & Wales)
- Branded invoices, receipts and documents
- Affiliate programme: refer-and-earn with a dedicated portal (payouts gated)

===================================================================
## ROLLING OUT — V2 functions (feature-flagged)
===================================================================
- Marketplace OS: a unified marketplace for property stays, suppliers/trades and
  emergency services — public listings, search and booking
- Bookings & accommodation: stay listings, reservations, availability, pricing,
  calendar and iCal sync
- Payments & escrow: Stripe Connect marketplace payments, escrow-style hold &
  release, deposits, refunds, disputes and seller payouts
- Customer workspace: a guest/customer area for bookings, stays, long-lets,
  saved properties, messages, reviews, payments and disputes
- Supplier workspace: jobs, quotes, invoicing, a tiered verification flow
  (identity/KYC, insurance, licences) and payouts
- Automations engine: triggers, nodes and full workflow automation
- Multi-country portfolio + global country packs
- Accounting GL and Making Tax Digital (MTD) support
- Seller verification gate (identity + insurance) enforced at marketplace launch

## Security & compliance
Server-enforced workspace isolation (Supabase RLS), server-side subscription
gating, signed private file storage, Stripe-verified billing webhooks, CSP/HSTS
security headers, rate limiting, encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, and
UK GDPR-aligned data handling.

## Payments & FCA position
Propvora-processed money (marketplace escrow, Connect payouts, its own
subscriptions) flows through Stripe. Tenant rent and directory-supplier payments
are RECORDED only — never collected or held by Propvora — keeping the platform
outside FCA-regulated money handling.
