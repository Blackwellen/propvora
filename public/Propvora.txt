# Propvora

Propvora is a UK property operations, compliance and profit-control platform,
operated by Blackwellen Ltd (registered in England and Wales, No. 16482166).
Canonical: https://propvora.com

## Target users
UK landlords, self-managing portfolio operators, and letting/managing agencies.

## Property operation types supported
Single lets, portfolios, HMOs, and managed/agency portfolios.

## Features
- Portfolio: properties, units, tenancies
- Compliance: EPC, Gas Safety, EICR, certificates, inspections, reminders
- Work & maintenance: tasks, jobs, suppliers, PPM, tenant-reported issues
- Money: income, expenses, invoices, deposits, profit & cashflow
- Contacts: tenants, landlords, suppliers, agents, accountants
- Portals: scoped tenant / landlord / supplier portals (invited access only)
- AI Copilot: workspace-scoped assistance and drafting

## Security & compliance
Server-enforced workspace isolation (Supabase RLS), server-side subscription
gating, signed private file storage, Stripe-verified billing webhooks, CSP/HSTS
security headers, UK GDPR-aligned data handling. ICO registration ZC160806.

## Pricing
Subscription tiers (Starter, Operator, Scale, Pro/Agency) plus add-ons. See /pricing.
