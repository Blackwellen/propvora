# Orbas CRM

> Orbas is a multi-app business operations platform built around a CRM core —
> "12 Business Apps. One Platform." It brings sales, finance, operations, people,
> service, projects, compliance and more into a single multi-tenant workspace with
> a built-in AI Copilot.

A Blackwellen Ltd product. Positioning from the product itself: 12 apps in one
platform with an AI Copilot across all of them and full audit trails.

## What it is
A unified business suite. Rather than separate CRM, accounting, HR, helpdesk and
project tools, Orbas ships these as integrated apps over one data model, workspace
model and admin/billing layer.

## Who it is for
Small-to-mid businesses and teams that want CRM plus back-office apps (finance, HR,
service, operations, projects) in one place, with role-based access and a client
portal. Plans referenced in-product scale from single users to unlimited-user tiers
with SSO/SAML and API access.

## The 12 apps (top-level modules found in src/app/(app))
1. CRM — leads, contacts, accounts, deals, pipeline, quotes, proposals,
   forecasting, campaigns, sequences, partners, territories, subscriptions
2. Accounting — invoices, bills, expenses, payments, banking, reconciliation,
   chart of accounts, journal entries, general ledger, budgets, multi-entity,
   multi-currency, tax, and UK Making Tax Digital (MTD VAT returns, ITSA),
   plus P&L / balance sheet / trial balance reports
3. Analytics — dashboards, report/dashboard builder, datasets, metrics, MRR,
   scheduled reports, exports
4. Automations — workflows, triggers, templates, run history, settings
5. Charity — donors, donations, pledges, campaigns, grants, gift aid, volunteers,
   impact and reports (non-profit module)
6. Compliance — risk register, controls, policies, audits, incidents, training,
   ESG/carbon reporting
7. Connect — omnichannel comms: email, social, DMs, live chat, routing, reports
8. Documents — contracts, e-signature requests, templates, shared docs, trash
9. Operations — assets, inventory, warehouses, purchase orders, work orders,
   field service, manufacturing, shipments, returns, quality, suppliers
10. People (HR) — directory, employees, hiring, leave/absence, payroll,
    performance, shifts, org chart, learning, benefits, compensation,
    workforce planning
11. Projects — projects, milestones, timesheets, resources, reports
12. Service — tickets, queues, SLAs, escalations, knowledge base, reports

Plus shared surfaces:
- Home: dashboard, AI briefing, approvals, inbox, tasks, work queue
- Client portal: documents, invoices, projects, support, profile (/portal/*)
- Admin: users, workspace, billing, integrations, apps, add-ons, API keys, audit
- Platform admin: tenant management (/platform-admin/*)
- Public e-sign route (/sign/[token]); marketing site (home, features, pricing,
  security, about, contact, book-demo, legal)
- Auth with MFA setup/recovery; onboarding (profile, workspace, team, apps)

## AI
Built-in "AI Copilot" promoted across all apps — ask questions, generate reports,
draft emails, summarise deals and assist with decisions, with audit trails. (Marketing
copy describes this as enterprise-grade AI; provider wiring is configured via env.)

## Tech stack (from package.json)
Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, Supabase (@supabase/ssr +
supabase-js), TanStack React Query, Radix UI primitives, react-hook-form + Zod,
Zustand, dnd-kit (drag/drop), cmdk (command palette), Recharts, next-themes,
lucide-react, date-fns.

## Status
v1.0 build line (package name "orbas-crm-final-build-v.1.0"). Extensive feature
surface is implemented as routed pages; some module depth and AI/provider wiring
depend on configuration.
