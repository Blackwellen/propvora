# Caption Fox

A Blackwellen Ltd product. Repository: https://github.com/Blackwellen/caption-fox

> Caption Fox is a social media management platform designed to streamline
> content creation, campaign management and brand monitoring for creators,
> small businesses, agencies and enterprises.

## What it is
A single-dashboard tool that aims to centralise social media workflows —
content planning, AI-assisted creation, scheduling/publishing, analytics and
team collaboration — so teams don't need several disconnected tools.

## Who it is for
- Creators managing multiple platforms
- Small businesses and brands running campaigns and monitoring brand presence
- Agencies managing multiple clients (with white-label/client-portal needs)
- Enterprise teams with approval workflows and compliance requirements

## Key features (planned/in development)
- AI-assisted caption generation and content creation
- Content calendar with scheduling and post-approval workflows
- Stock media library and image generation integrations
- Social listening / brand-mention tracking with sentiment analysis
- Campaign and giveaway management with ROI tracking
- Unified inbox across messaging and social channels
- Analytics, custom dashboards and report generation
- Role-based permissions, multi-workspace and agency/white-label features
- Link-in-bio landing pages

## Tech stack
Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase. Deployed (preview) at https://caption-fox.vercel.app

## Status
Early-stage / in active development. The feature set above is drawn from the
project's implementation plan and should be treated as planned scope, not a
list of shipped, production-ready features.
