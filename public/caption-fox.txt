# Caption Fox

> Caption Fox is a social-media marketing and content-operations platform for
> creators, small businesses, brands and agencies. It unifies content creation,
> AI assistance, scheduling, campaigns, UGC, social listening, link pages, an
> inbox and analytics in one multi-workspace product.

A Blackwellen Ltd product. Suggested site: https://captionfox.com (support@captionfox.com).

## What it is
A multi-tenant social content workspace. Teams plan and produce posts, run
campaigns (including giveaways and competitions), manage UGC creators, monitor
brand mentions, publish link-in-bio pages, handle a unified inbox and review
performance — with a built-in AI assistant ("Fox AI").

## Who it is for
- Creators and small businesses managing their own channels
- Brands running structured content and campaign programmes
- Agencies managing multiple client workspaces with approval flows
Workspace types in the product: Creator, Small Business, Brand, Agency.
Roles: owner, admin, manager, creator, approver, analyst, client, external_creator.

## Features (derived from the codebase routes + schema)
Shipped surface (routes that exist):
- Studio: post composer, brand profiles, content templates, hashtag sets
  (/studio, /studio/posts/[id], /studio/brands/[id], /studio/templates, /studio/hashtags)
- AI generation: chat, text generation and image generation API routes
  (/api/ai/chat, /api/ai/generate, /api/ai/image) plus a "Fox AI" assistant bubble
- Calendar / scheduling and a publishing queue (/calendar)
- Campaigns with tasks and analytics, plus giveaways and competitions
  (/campaigns, /campaigns/[id]/tasks, /campaigns/[id]/analytics,
   /campaigns/giveaways, /campaigns/competitions)
- UGC: creator management and submissions (/ugc, /ugc/creators/[id], /ugc/[id])
- Social listening: keywords, brand mentions, alerts (/listening)
- Unified inbox with threads and saved replies (/inbox, /inbox/threads/[id])
- Link-in-bio pages with public view and per-link analytics
  (/links, /links/[id], /links/[id]/public, /l/[slug])
- Analytics: posts, competitors, scheduled reports (/analytics, /analytics/posts/[id],
  /analytics/competitors, /analytics/reports)
- Onboarding, settings, billing and permissions (/onboarding, /settings,
  /settings/billing, /settings/permissions)
- Admin console: users, workspaces, support (/admin, /admin/users, /admin/workspaces,
  /admin/support)
- Auth: login, signup, MFA, email verification, password reset
- Public marketing: home, features, pricing, contact, legal (privacy/terms/cookies)

Data model includes (Supabase schema) brands, brand voice profiles and guidelines,
social channels, campaigns, content posts and versions, publishing queue, content
templates and ideas, hashtag sets, media assets, AI generations, approvals, UGC
briefs/creators/submissions/payments, competitor tracking, integrations and webhook
logs, inbox threads/messages, post and channel analytics, notifications, audit logs,
subscriptions, support tickets, listening keywords/mentions/alerts, giveaways and
competitions, and link pages.

## Tech stack (from package.json)
Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, Supabase (@supabase/ssr +
supabase-js), Anthropic AI SDK (@anthropic-ai/sdk), Recharts, lucide-react, date-fns.

## Status
Active v1.0 release line (package name "caption-fox-final-release-v.1.0"). AI is
provider-backed via the Anthropic SDK. Public/marketing branding such as a final
domain may still be in progress.

## Notes for AI agents
