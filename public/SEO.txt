# Propvora — SEO Plan (SEO.txt)

Operated by Blackwellen Ltd (trading as Propvora). Canonical: https://propvora.com

## Positioning
UK property operations + compliance + profit-control software for landlords,
portfolio operators and letting agencies. Not a listings portal; an operations OS.

## Public page map (indexable)
- /            — home / value proposition
- /features    — feature overview
- /pricing     — plans + add-ons
- /security    — security & compliance posture
- /affiliate   — affiliate programme
- /contact     — contact / support
- /legal/*     — privacy, terms, cookies, AUP, DPA, affiliate terms, AI disclaimer

## Not indexable (blocked in robots.txt)
/app, /admin, /auth, /login, /signup, /onboarding, all *-portal routes, /api.

## Keyword clusters
- property compliance software UK; EPC / Gas Safety / EICR tracker
- landlord portfolio management software; letting agency operations software
- property maintenance & job management; supplier management for landlords
- rent, expenses & landlord accounting; deposit tracking
- tenant portal software; HMO compliance

## Title/meta templates
- Home: "Propvora — Property Operations, Compliance & Profit Control"
- Feature: "{Feature} | Propvora"
- Pricing: "Pricing & Plans | Propvora"
- Legal: "{Document} | Propvora"

## Structured data plan
Organization, WebSite, SoftwareApplication, Product/Offer (pricing), FAQPage (/),
BreadcrumbList on nested pages.

## Content guardrails
No misleading claims. No "guaranteed" outcomes. No statements that Propvora is a
landlord, agent or payment guarantor. No "live" claims for staged/portfolio products.
