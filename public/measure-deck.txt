# MeasureDeck

> MeasureDeck gives construction quantity-surveying and commercial teams a single
> platform to manage valuations, Cost Value Reconciliation (CVR), change events,
> final accounts and cost reporting — with a live, always-current commercial
> picture instead of spreadsheet version conflicts.

A Blackwellen Ltd product. Suggested site: https://measuredeck.com.

## What it is
A commercial management system for construction projects. It replaces
"CVR_FINAL_v3_JamesEdits_ACTUAL.xlsx"-style spreadsheets with a live workspace that
pulls costs and value together, links dispute evidence, and tracks the full
commercial lifecycle of a project.

## Who it is for
Contractors, quantity surveyors, commercial managers and project managers. Designed
around common UK construction contracts: JCT (SBC, D&B, Minor Works), NEC3/NEC4 and
FIDIC. Each workspace supports unlimited projects under one subscription, with
portfolio-level KPIs for commercial directors.

## Features (derived from routes + Supabase migrations)
- Projects with project contracts and per-project commercial views
  (/projects, /projects/[projectId], /cvr/[projectId])
- Cost Value Reconciliation (CVR): periods, lines, risks — a live CVR
  (cvr_periods, cvr_lines, cvr_risks)
- Change events / variations with pricing (change_events, change_event_pricing)
  (/changes, /changes/[changeId])
- Payment applications & certifications: applications, application lines,
  certifications, payment records (/applications, /applications/[applicationId])
- Final accounts with line items (/final-accounts, final_accounts, final_account_lines)
- Evidence management: files and links binding documents to commercial items,
  plus an upload flow (/evidence, /evidence/upload, evidence_files, evidence_links)
- Drawings register with revisions and a drawing viewer
  (/drawings, /drawings/[drawingId]/viewer, drawing_register, drawing_revisions)
- BIM models (/bim/models, /bim/[modelId], bim_models)
- Programme / schedule with a Gantt view (/schedule, /schedule/gantt, schedule_items)
- Site map layers and markers (site_map_layers, site_map_markers)
- Risk register, contract documents and dayworks sheets (risk_register,
  contract_documents, daywork_sheets)
- Suppliers, contacts and tasks with comments/links (/suppliers, /contacts, /tasks)
- Reports and exports (/reports, /reports/[reportId], reports, report_exports)
- Built-in AI chat assistant: "MeasureDeck AI", a construction-commercial assistant
  for CVR, payment applications, change events, EoT/delay, retention, final accounts
  and JCT/NEC/FIDIC guidance (/api/ai/chat) — backed by an AI usage ledger and
  AI action requests (ai_usage_ledger, ai_action_requests)
- Inbox threads/messages, notifications, audit log, feature-flag overrides
- Account, billing, settings and workspace settings; onboarding; auth with MFA
- Admin console: users, workspaces, subscriptions, plans, modules, AI usage,
  commercial, drawings, files, storage, integrations, security, health, release, legal
- Public marketing + legal: features, pricing, security, demo, waitlist, affiliate,
  AI disclaimer, privacy/terms/cookies/DPA/subprocessors/acceptable-use/refund

## Tech stack (from package.json + README)
Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, Supabase (@supabase/ssr +
supabase-js), TanStack React Query + Table + Virtual, Radix UI primitives,
react-hook-form + Zod, dnd-kit, Recharts, framer-motion, react-dropzone, sonner.
Billing: Stripe (@stripe/stripe-js + stripe). AI: OpenAI (OPENAI_API_KEY /
OPENAI_MODEL). Email: Resend. File storage: Cloudflare R2. UK-region Supabase hosting.
