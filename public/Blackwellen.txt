# Blackwellen Ltd

Blackwellen Ltd is a UK software company registered in England and Wales
(company number 16482166; registered office 61 Bridge Street, Kington, HR5 3DJ).
ICO Data Protection registration: ZC160806. Site: blackwellen.com / blackwellen.co.uk.

## Positioning
Blackwellen is building a focused family of vertical SaaS products on a shared,
audit-grade platform for operations-heavy industries. Each product is a deep
vertical app, but they share common platform traits: built-in workflow automation,
structured records, role-based access, observability, and internal AI capabilities.

## Products (real focus, one line each)
- Orbas CRM — multi-app business operations suite ("12 apps, one platform"): CRM,
  accounting, analytics, automations, charity, compliance, comms, documents,
  operations, people/HR, projects and service, with a built-in AI Copilot.
  See orbas-crm.txt
- Propvora — UK property operations, compliance and profit-control platform for
  landlords, portfolio operators and letting agencies. Canonical: https://propvora.com
  See Propvora.txt
- CertificateFlow — compliance, governance and assurance operating system for
  regulated organisations (business, charity, school/MAT, faith, audit firms),
  with proof states, an assurance score and an enterprise control plane.
  See certificate-flow.txt
- MeasureDeck — construction commercial-management / quantity-surveying platform:
  live CVR, valuations, change events, final accounts and cost reporting
  (JCT / NEC / FIDIC). See measure-deck.txt
- Gala Dock — events and venue management platform: leads, events, suppliers/RFQs,
  venues and bookings, finance, forms, library and portals. See gala-dock.txt
- Caption Fox — social-media marketing and content-operations platform: content
  studio, AI assistance, scheduling, campaigns, UGC, listening, inbox, link pages
  and analytics. See caption-fox.txt

## Shared platform traits
- Built-in workflow automation
- Structured records and audit-ready trails
- Role-based access and multi-tenant workspaces
- Observability (audit logs, run/job health, usage)
- Internal AI capabilities (assistive, configuration-gated, with audit trails)

Common stack across the family: Next.js / React (CertificateFlow uses Vite + React),
TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Supabase (Postgres, Auth, Storage), Stripe billing where
applicable.
