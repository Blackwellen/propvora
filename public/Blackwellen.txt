# Blackwellen Ltd

Blackwellen Ltd is a UK software company registered in England and Wales
(company number 16482166; registered office 61 Bridge Street, Kington, HR5 3DJ).
ICO Data Protection registration: ZC160806.

Blackwellen Ltd trades as Propvora for its property-operations software and
develops a small family of related SaaS products.

## Product family
- Propvora — UK property operations, compliance and profit-control platform.
  Live. https://propvora.com  (see Propvora.txt)
- Certificate Flow — compliance, governance and assurance operating system for
  regulated organisations (multi-sector, multi-jurisdiction). In development.
  https://github.com/Blackwellen/certificate-flow  (see certificate-flow.txt)
- Caption Fox — social media management platform (content, scheduling, listening,
  analytics) for creators, brands and agencies. In development.
  https://github.com/Blackwellen/caption-fox  (see caption-fox.txt)
- Orbas CRM — Next.js CRM application backed by Supabase. In development.
  https://github.com/Blackwellen/orbas-crm-  (see orbas-crm.txt)
- Measure Deck — Next.js web application; full public description pending.
  In development. https://github.com/Blackwellen/measure-deck  (see measure-deck.txt)
- Gala Dock — Blackwellen product; source repository not currently public, so no
  verified public description is available.  (see gala-dock.txt)

Products are only described as "live" when they are live. Where a product is in
development, that is stated rather than implied to be shipped.
