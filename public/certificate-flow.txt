# Certificate Flow

A Blackwellen Ltd product. Repository: https://github.com/Blackwellen/certificate-flow

> A compliance, governance and assurance operating system for regulated
> organisations — built for businesses, charities, schools, multi-academy
> trusts, faith bodies and audit firms across the UK, EU, US, Canada,
> Australia and New Zealand.

## What it is
Certificate Flow is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that helps regulated
organisations operationalise compliance, governance and assurance work in one
place — turning obligations, controls and evidence into a tracked, audit-ready
workflow.

## Who it is for
Businesses, charities, schools, multi-academy trusts, faith bodies and audit
firms in the UK, EU, US, Canada, Australia and New Zealand.

## Key features
- Proof-first action plane with evidence-state tracking
- Continuous assurance scoring across compliance domains
- Audit-ready immutable event logging with watermarked exports
- Sector-specific compliance packs
- AI co-pilot for drafting, summarisation and field extraction
- Risk registers, control libraries and obligations tracking
- DSAR pipelines and privacy management
- Enterprise SSO (SAML 2.0) and SCIM provisioning
- Stripe billing with multi-currency support

## Tech stack
React, Vite, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui; Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth,
Storage, Edge Functions); Stripe for billing; pluggable AI providers; Vitest
for testing.

## Status
In active development. Features above reflect the repository's documented scope.

## Relationship to Propvora
Certificate Flow is a separate, broader multi-sector compliance/assurance
product. Propvora is a UK property-operations and compliance platform; the two
share a compliance focus but target different users and are not the same product.
