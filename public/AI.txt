# Propvora — AI Usage Notice (AI.txt)

Operated by Blackwellen Ltd (trading as Propvora). Canonical: https://propvora.com

## AI features
- AI Copilot (chat): answers questions and drafts text using ONLY the signed-in
  user's current workspace data.
- AI quick actions: portfolio summaries, missing-document checks, arrears guidance,
  priority lists, cashflow framing, draft letters.

## Boundaries & limitations
- Workspace-scoped: the Copilot can only see the authenticated user's active
  workspace. It cannot read other workspaces, other tenants' data, or admin context.
- No final advice: Propvora AI does not provide final legal, tax or financial advice.
  Outputs are drafts/assistance and require human review.
- No autonomous risky actions: the AI does not delete data, send external email,
  make payments, or submit to HMRC without explicit user approval.
- Untrusted content: uploaded files and external text are treated as untrusted;
  the AI will not follow instructions embedded in them to leak or alter data.

## Data & privacy
- Prompts/responses may be logged for usage metering and abuse prevention under the
  workspace's permission controls. See /legal/privacy and /legal/ai-disclaimer.
- AI usage is rate-limited and cost-capped per user/workspace/plan.

## Model/provider
- Provider/model disclosure is maintained in the in-app AI settings and the
  subprocessor register. Contact: https://propvora.com/contact
