## Pattern / queued
- **Common everywhere with tourism:** registration number on listings + platform verification + municipal night caps + change-of-use/planning. The pack stores: `requiresRegistration`, `registrationName`, `nightCap`, `authority`, `planningChangeOfUse`.
- **Queued:** Greece (AMA registry), AU (state — e.g. NSW 180-day non-hosted cap), US cities (NYC Local Law 18, SF, LA), Japan (minpaku 180-day Housing Accommodation Business Act), Ireland (STTL register + RPZ planning), Croatia (tourism category), Saudi (tourism licence), Thailand (Hotel Act <30-day restriction).

> **Encoding note:** dim 10 drives the **applicability + cost + compliance** of the Serviced Accommodation and Holiday Lets planning profiles, and a "short-let registration" record type in Legal/Compliance. Night caps + change-of-use are the highest-divergence levers. Many are dated 2024-2026 → configurable.
